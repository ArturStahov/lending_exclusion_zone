History Exclusion Zone

The game about Chernobyl exclusion zone 30 years after the Chernobyl accident.   Zombie apocalypse started here!   Due to the disaster that occurred on the territory of the former URSR in 1986 at the Chernobyl nuclear power plant, a huge part of the territory was evacuated by creating the so-called Exclusion Zone.   After 30 years, due to the action of increased radiation, unexplained facts began to occur in the form of mutations of living flesh. In the zone, experiments and studies on the development of mutagens began, but at one point it became impossible to control the mutation.   Under your control, the post, the former laboratory, is equipped with a workshop, a warehouse, a generator and even transport.   Your main goal is to prevent a crowd of zombie mutants from breaking into your shelter. To do this, you need to provide the post with provisions making forays into the zone. Survive at any cost!!!   The main resource for survival - ammunition, they also became the main currency in the new world. You will also need a lot of effort to extract clean water and fuel for your generator and transport. For the good, you have a workshop where it is possible, in the presence of various kinds of components, to make this or that resource.   You need the resources to upgrade your defenses, vehicles, weapons.   Develop your post “Shelter”, not quick, it will not be easy to do this - but such is the price to survive.   Develop your character by increasing his level and characteristics. You can choose one of the specializations.
